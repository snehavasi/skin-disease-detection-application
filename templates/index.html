ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

Skin diseases are common yet complex, requiring early detection for effective treatment. Skin Disease Classification is an AI-powered web application designed to assist users in identifying and understanding skin conditions with ease.

Our website leverages advanced deep learning techniques, specifically the VGG-16 model with transfer learning, to accurately classify three major skin diseases based on uploaded images. By simply providing a skin lesion image, users receive an instant classification along with relevant information.

These skin diseases are:
  • Actinic
  • Acne
  • Melanoma
